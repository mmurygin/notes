## Object Pronouns

| pronouns | object   | english         |
|----------|----------|-----------------|
| ik       | me / mij | me              |
| je / jij | je, jou  | your (informal) |
| u        | u        | your (formal)   |
| he / hij | hem      | him             |
| ze / zij | haar     | her             |
| we       | ons      | us              |
| jullie   | jullie   | you             |
| ze / zij | ze / hen | them            |
|----------|----------|-----------------|


## Possessive Pronouns

| pronouns | possessive | english         |
|----------|------------|-----------------|
